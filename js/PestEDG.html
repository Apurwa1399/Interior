Life @ PestEDGE

Our Staff, are our Pride! They are significant to the entirely we do at PestEDGE and they are an integral part of our PestEDGE Family. We strongly believe nurturing and developing talent thereby getting the best out of them and unleashing their potential to create an elegance organization. We continued to optimize and improve the efficiency of the Company’s business processes. Our team includes trained professionals who are enthusiastic and passionate about their work. Being a Socially Accountable Employer we believe and encourage in diversity and provide Equal Opportunity for both male and female employees. Life at PestEDGE is about diversity and inclusion without a discrimination on the basis of gender, religion, caste, creed, demography, etc.

We are seeking for to hire the best professionals in the field regardless of their age and gender and are ready to develop their skills further and provide the necessary education and qualifications.

Our policy is aimed towards attracting and retaining distinctly qualified, certified and efficient specialist staff. We strive to create a productive working environment and open up new opportunities for personnel to realize their professional and personal potential in line with the Company’s strategy.

We believe that every PestEDGE’ian has the potential to make a positive difference to the organization, community at large. As we nurture talent, it is important that we reward meritocracy and recognize outstanding talent through a host of Reward & Recognition Programs across the company.

Let’s JOIN US! Get on board for an exciting journey!
